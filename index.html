Seaching for Data Sets

Synopsis:

The cause that I have chosen is a combination of sports and diversity. As professional athletes have recently participated in numerous social causes, it reminds crazed fans and casual spectators of all professional/collegiate sports, that these athletes are very similar to us in their backgrounds and have originated from common upbringings. In my search for data sets, I was able to come across reports done by The Institute for Diversity and Ethics in Sport (TIDES) about the various sports leagues and media organizations across the U.S. Their 'Racial and Gender Report Cards' assess the diversity in these leagues from the players all the way up to ownership. The grades that they give out are based upon the percentages of different ethnicities and genders in the league compared to that of the demographics shown by the U.S. Census. From this data, I hope to help visualize the discrepency in the percentages of players of color compared to that of coaches and owners of color. An issue that can go unseen by the casual fan's eye.
